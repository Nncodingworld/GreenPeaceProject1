About Us

At Green Peace, we’re passionate about promoting sustainable plant care practices that contribute to a healthier planet. Our expert team provides personalized solutions to help your plants thrive, with a focus on eco-friendly methods. Join us in cultivating a greener future, one plant at a time

Our Mission & Vision is to provide expert plant care guidance that promotes sustainability and helps individuals and businesses cultivate healthy, thriving plants in an eco-friendly way. We envision a world where everyone embraces sustainable plant care practices, fostering greener spaces that contribute to a healthier environment for future generations.

  • Personalized plant care with eco-friendly solutions.

  • Expert advice on plant health and gardening.

  • Empowering greener, healthier spaces for all.

Our Services

Greenhouse & Hydroponics Consulting

Offer guidance for clients interested in setting up or optimizing a greenhouse or hydroponic system, focusing on improving plant growth in these environments.

Indoor Plant Care & Urban Gardening Advice

Provide tips and strategies for growing plants indoors, in small spaces, or in urban environments. This could include advice on selecting plants suitable for low-light conditions or maximizing space for small apartment gardening.

Custom Garden Design (for Outdoor Plants)

Offer garden planning and design services, including recommendations for plant selection, layout, and how to create a sustainable, low-maintenance garden.
